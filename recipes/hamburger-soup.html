Hamburger Soup

Description

This hearty hamburger soup offers a delightful blend of textures and flavors, combining savory ground beef with a vibrant array of vegetables. Each spoonful is a warm embrace, showcasing the rich, comforting essence of a classic meal. The soup's inviting aroma fills the kitchen, promising a nourishing experience that warms the soul. Perfectly paired with homemade bread, this dish transforms any mealtime into a cozy gathering, where every bite is both satisfying and wholesome. Its versatility allows for easy customization, making it a beloved staple for families seeking comfort in a bowl.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen corn kernals
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 6 beef bouillon cubes
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease.
  3. Combine beef, water, tomatoes, corn, tomato sauce, carrots, celery, onion, bouillon, ketchup, basil, and salt in a large stockpot; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for at least 1 Â½ hours.
